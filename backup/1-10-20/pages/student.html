Academic Year 20/21

Welcome to Ulster University. Whether you are joining us for the first time or returning to your studies, you can be assured of our commitment to delivering the best possible experience for our students, whether online or on campus.

Despite the challenges presented by the ongoing restrictions, we will continue to make it possible for you to engage in your programme of study in the most meaningful and effective ways.

As we re-imagine University life alongside this pandemic, you can look forward to vibrant, inspiring and productive time, and we look forward to welcoming you.

Find out more about teaching delivery and campus services for Semester 1 along with the measures we are taking to support you to keep studying and keep safe on campus.

Useful sites

Registry

If you need advice about your student record, student finance or completing forms that require official university approval, Registry can offer you support and guidance. If you are unsure about the service you need, contact Registry and we will ensure your question gets answered.

Fees and Finance

The Fees Office can support you with any queries regarding your tuition fees from setting up a payment plan to dealing with sponsor letters and much more.

ISD

The Information Services Department are your IT gurus and will support you with all your technical needs, from advice regarding technology for online learning to support with University systems.

Library

Library services provide resources to support learning, teaching and research. The team will assist you with ebooks as well as guide you on library opening times.

Student Wellbeing

Student Wellbeing provide a range of services to support you during your studies to include mental health and wellbeing, disability, counselling and a sexual health clinic. Contact us if you need support, our friendly team will provide guidance and support.

Employability

Employability services provide a range of courses designed with your career in mind, with most developed in partnership with employers to ensure the skills, knowledge and experience you gain match the needs of your chosen profession. Contact us for more information on how we can support you.

Accommodation

ResLife can provide you with the information you need on the location, quality and price of accommodation across the campuses. Information is available on the services provided and the range of support, activities and events available to residential students.

Sports Services

The sports centres offer world class facilities that are open all year round. We offer a range of classes and a competitive membership fee. Get in touch to find out more about what we have to offer.

Estates/ Car Parking

Estates services can guide you on car parking enquiries, access control as well as providing a safezone app for your safety on campus.

UUSU

Think of the Students’ Union as your university family! Led by student officers, UUSU ensures that you are represented. We offer a helping hand whenever you might need us and we bring fun and frolics to your student experience. You are automatically a member so go get involved at uusu.org

Doctoral College

The Doctoral College supports PhD Researchers navigate the registration and enrolment process at Ulster and we can answer any queries you may have. We can guide your through our online PhD Management System (PhD Manager) and help you engage with the many Researcher Development Programme (RDP) training sessions that we offer.

Go Global

Study abroad allows you to undertake part of your undergraduate degree outside of the UK at one of our exchange partner universities.

Student Charter

Our Student Charter explains what you can expect from the University and what we expect of our students.
