Ulster University Qatar

Ulster University is a multi-campus university tracing its origins back to1845, with sights set firmly on the future. The university has now spread beyond its Northern Ireland roots to include branch campuses in London, Birmingham, and most recently in Qatar.

Our Qatar branch is operated in partnership with City College, with a new state-of-the-art campus undergoing construction in the “smart city” of Lusail. Students graduating from Qatar branch will get a globally recognised, fully accredited UK degree from Ulster University, licensed by the Ministry of Education and Higher Education in Qatar.
