          Postgraduate Dentistry Courses

          New provision in postgraduate dentistry at Ulster University.

          Apply now for September 2019 entry!

          Out-centre arrangement for new suite of full-time and part-time MSc postgraduate programmes, delivered by Ulster University’s School of Pharmacy & Pharmaceutical Sciences with the College of Medicine and Dentistry in Birmingham.

          ‘Career focused and highly relevant to today’s dental professionals, providing specialist skills in clinical dentistry’

          Source : https://comd.org.uk/

          Full-time Courses

          We deliver a number of Master of Science (MSc) post-graduate dentistry courses that are career focused and highly relevant to today’s dental professionals, providing specialist skills in clinical dentistry.

          Further details are provided in the relevant courses below.

          Advanced Clinical and Diagnostic Oral Sciences - MSc

          The course aims to develop confident senior dental practitioners to undertake effective evidence-base practice in diagnostic oral sciences.

          Full-time Postgraduate course

          Year of entry: 2019/20

          College of Dentistry, Birmingham

          UCAS: W200
