Before you apply

Information for international applicants: what you need to know before you apply.

Welcome

Whether you are lo​o​king for undergraduate​ or postgraduate study, Ulster University is an excellent choice for international students choosing to study in the UK.​

Ulster University is home to approximately 27,000 students, with over 1200 international students from more than 143 countries. Our international students are value​d and respected for the unique experiences and perspectives that they contribute to university life, enriching the study experience for all students.

Our courses are designed with your career in mind, with most developed in partnership with employers to ensure the skills, knowledge and experience you gain match the needs of your chosen profession.

There are a range of study opportunities available to you: undergraduate, postgraduate, research, short-term study abroad and exchange options.

Before you apply have a look at our online prospectus to check that you are likely to meet any course-specific entry requirements. Please also check our general entry requirements.

My international experience at Ulster University has been unbelievable. To be able to learn in a variety of contexts and be able to adapt is a great asset for future career and life settings. I feel that the diference will make me a stronger learner and a greater competitor in my future career.

Chelsea Pietraallo, USA International exchange student

Tuition Fees

The annual tuition fee for most of our undergraduate and postgraduate programmes is £13,680 (GBP) per academic year. Tuition fees are subject to change and are reviewed on an annual basis. Please check your course information and our Tuition Fees page for information specific to your course.

Country-specific information

Visit our country-specific pages to find out the minimum requirements, educational representatives near you and meet our Global Recruitment and Engagement team. We work with a number of educational advisers across the globe, who can assist you to make an application to study at Ulster.

International Foundation Programme (IFP)

Not meet our general entry requirements for undergraduate study? Choose our International Foundation Programme to help you get your undergraduate degree.

Study Abroad in the UK

You don't have to sutdy with us on a full-time 3 year programme, you can also sample university life in the UK by studying one of over 2,500 modules at Ulster University over 6-12 months. Check out our Study Abroad options.

eLearning

Applications to our eLearning full-time and part-time courses are made directly using our online application system . You can apply for up to nine eLearning courses in any one academic year. Application closing dates vary by course but we try to be flexible in allowing applications to be made up until the start of the course.

Accommodation Guarantee

We guarantee a room in University-managed accommodation to all of our international applicants, as long as you have reserved your room by 31 July.

International Admissions Service

We want to make the admissions process as smooth as possible for our international students. Staff within the International Admissions Service are here to guide you through the process.

Got a question? Email us: internationaladmissions@ulster.ac.uk
