    NI, GB and EU Students

    What to bring to your Admissions Check-in

    You will require the following documentation:

    1. Photographic ID (e.g. Passport or Driving Licence)
    2. Documentary evidence of all examinations taken from GCSE Level (or equivalent) onwards if not already provided to the University.

      You will not be able to enrol online until this information has been provided.

      You can also email admissions@ulster.ac.uk with copies of your qualifications. Please include your UCAS ID when providing this.

    3. If not already enrolled, you will need to bring payment for fees; or acceptable evidence that fees will be paid by an education authority or sponsor.

    Normally if you are enrolling on a postgraduate course (with the exception of PGCE courses), you will only be required to produce evidence relating to your primary degree (unless otherwise specifically requested by the University).

    International students

    Tier 4 students

    You must bring:

    1. Your passport
    2. Your CAS statement
    3. Your Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) if you have received this
    4. Your academic qualification/s (this is listed in the “Documents Used to Obtain Offer” section of your CAS statement)
    5. Your English language qualification (e.g. IELTS certificate, WAEC certificate, Indian XII certificate)

    You may also need to bring:

    1. Evidence of the date on which you arrived in the UK (this could be a copy of your boarding pass or travel ticket)*
    2. Evidence from the UK Home Office that you have a Tier 4 visa application in progress**

    * This is only needed if you entered the UK through the eGates or if you did not receive an entry stamp in your passport when you went through immigration control in the UK.

    ** This is only needed if you have submitted an application for Tier 4 Further Leave to Remain in the UK and you have not yet received your Tier 4 visa for studying at Ulster University

    Other International students

    You must bring:

    1. Your passport
    2. Your BRP or other evidence of your UK immigration status (if applicable)

    You may also need to bring:

    1. Evidence of the date on which you arrived in the UK (this could be a copy of your boarding pass or travel ticket)*

    * This is only needed if you entered the UK through the eGates or if you did not receive an entry stamp in your passport when you went through immigration control in the UK.

    You will not be permitted to complete your registration if you do not bring these documents to registration.
