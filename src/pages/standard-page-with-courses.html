          Postgraduate study in Pharmacy

          View our options for postgraduate study in Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences.

          Ranked as the UK's number 1 university for Pharmacy and Pharmacology (Guardian League Tables 2017, 2016 and 2015)

          Our graduates come from around the world, all walks of life and have studied a variety of subjects.

          You can view more information on eLearning options on our Ulster Life section.

          Pharmacy Courses

          Our two postgraduate courses are studied by eLearning, which enables you to learn remotely and offers flexibility to study at your own pace, any place and any time.

          Pharmacy Management - MSc

          MSc Pharmacy Management offers pharmacy practitioners the opportunity to gain a unique qualification to enhance their career development.

          Part-time Postgraduate course   |   Year of entry: 2019/20  |  

          View course      Watch video  

          Pharmaceutical Sciences - PgDip/MSc

          This programme is designed to provide an up-to-date knowledge and understanding of core areas of pharmaceutical sciences.

          Part-time Postgraduate course   |   Year of entry: 2019/20  |  

          View course      Watch video  

          Pharmaceutical Sciences - PgDip/MSc

          This programme is designed to provide an up-to-date knowledge and understanding of core areas of pharmaceutical manufacturing and research.
