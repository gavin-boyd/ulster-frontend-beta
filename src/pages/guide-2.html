    Loyalty Points

    On each visit to the Wellness Suite, fitness class or squash courts, the person paying will be rewarded with 20 loyalty points and once sufficient points have been accrued they can be redeemed at reception.

    Your membership must be valid in order to redeem these points.

    Terms and conditions

    • Automatic entry to loyalty scheme for members
    • Loyalty points awarded for first/only session of any given day to the person paying for the activity
    • Points may be redeemed for fitness classes and sessions.
