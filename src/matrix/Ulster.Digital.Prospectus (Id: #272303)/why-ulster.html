Why Ulster?

Going to university is an exciting step and choosing where to study is an important decision that will shape your future.

Ulster University is based in Northern Ireland, with four distinct campuses in Belfast, Coleraine, Jordanstown and Derry~Londonderry (Magee).

The student experience we offer is second to none. From great teaching to first class resources and facilities, all within a supportive learning environment, you will receive a ‘uniquely Ulster’ experience that will help you to fulfil your potential.
