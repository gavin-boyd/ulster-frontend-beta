    Staging - COVID-19 (Coronavirus)

    The welfare of our students and staff is a key priority for Ulster University. We recognise that this may be an anxious time for our University community. On these pages you will find advice and information for students and staff about COVID-19, in line with official Public Health Agency guidance. We are continuing to monitor the evolving situation and will provide the latest updates and guidance here.

    Staying
    up to date

    As we respond to the impact of COVID we are ensuring that students and staff are fully informed of the latest official advice from the Public Health Agency (NI), and continue to issue and update advice and guidance on this website.

    Email

    We routinely communicate with staff and students by email to share important ‘Need to know’ information or guidance and will continue to do so.

    Text Alert

    We are also able to alert all students via text message that will direct you to our website for the latest information.

    To support us to reach you in this way, we would ask you to check and update your mobile contact details held on Banner.

    Similarly, staff can avail of text alerts by updating their mobile details on Core HR. Provision of mobile details is entirely optional, but will enable us to actively draw your attention to important notifications.

    Twitter

    You are also encouraged to follow us on Twitter @UlsterUni, and to regularly check this website, where you will find information via the COVID-19 banner at the top of the home page.
