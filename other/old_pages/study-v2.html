Study options

Whatever your ambition, at Ulster University we can offer a way of learning that works with your life. Whether you are considering undergraduate or postgraduate study, you will join a vibrant student community where there are new friendships to be made, ideas to be shared and opportunities to be explored.

Undergraduate

Fulfil your potential and shape your future with an undergraduate degree from Ulster University.

Postgraduate

Gain the knowledge, skills and confidence to enhance your career with a postgraduate course at Ulster University.

Postgraduate Research

We have numerous research opportunities open to applicants from home and overseas.

Short courses and CPD

We provide high-quality short courses for everyone. Whether you want to improve your job prospects, learn new skills, get a qualification or develop workplace training, we can help.

Part-time

Take control of your learning journey and study at a pace that suits you with a flexible part-time course at Ulster University.

eLearning

Join over 4,000 students who have graduated from Ulster University with an online degree or short course qualification - eLearning courses offer flexibility enabling you to study at your own pace, any place and any time.
