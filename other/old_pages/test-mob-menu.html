    Collab Lab at Coleraine campus

    A creative and flexible learning space providing a platform for you to work together, to solve problems and develop new ideas.

    Whether you are a student, staff member or member of the local community we welcome you to use this space to collaborate and be creative – team work makes the dream work!

    Designed to be used by a diverse range of people, the Collab Lab is accessible to a variety of users including (but not limited to):

    • Academic staff delivering modules focused on industry and community collaboration
    • PhD researchers focused on engaging industry and professional body organisations
    • Public sector, private, professional and community organisations
    • Staff engaged in campus initiatives (e.g. Community Choir, Women’s Network)

    We hope this space will allow you to start the conversations that will make a difference.
